"Bangladesh’s recent people’s movement, led by our youth, has been crucial in reshaping our nation. They resisted autocracy, oppression, and corruption, demanding democracy and justice, even at great personal cost. This resolve defines our future as a responsive, responsible state.We are committed to rebuilding Bangladesh, focusing on good governance, justice, and fundamental rights. Reforms in electoral, judicial, and civil systems are underway. Bangladesh remains dedicated to multilateralism and peace, contributing actively to UN peacekeeping operations. As we confront global challenges such as climate change, migration, and economic inequality, Bangladesh calls for collective action to ensure a just and prosperous future for all."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
